Kingdoms CCG is a high fantasy collectible card game inspired by popular tabletop equivalents. Card combat was augmented with powerful hero characters the player chose for each deck, along with lane mechanics to split up the battlefield.

Highlights

  • Pen-and-paper prototyping potential cards
  • Developing a robust AI system for allowing the computer to play arbitrary future cards intelligently that is still in use today
  • Collaborating with the team on the design of the cards
  • Building out the architecture for the product
